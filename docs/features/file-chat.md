# Chat with PDFs

Upload a paper and ask questions about its contents. The AI reads the PDF and answers based on what’s inside.

---

## Prerequisites

- A `MOONSHOT_API_KEY` or other AI backend configured (admin-only)
- A PDF uploaded to Resources (File → Upload)

---

## How to Chat

1. Go to **Resources**
2. Find the uploaded PDF
3. Click the **message bubble** icon
4. Type a question: “What methodology did they use?” or “Summarize the main findings”
5. Press Enter

The answer appears within a few seconds, with citations to the source pages.

---

## What Works Best

- Summarizing a section (“Explain the experiment in simple terms”)
- Extracting data (“What sample size did they use?”)
- Clarifying definitions (“How do they define X?”)
- Finding specific information (“What repository do they use?”)

---

## Limitations

- Scanned PDFs (no text layer) won’t work
- Very long PDFs (>100 pages) may be truncated
- The AI can make mistakes—always verify with the original

---

## Privacy

PDF content is sent to the configured AI API. If you need maximum privacy, avoid uploading sensitive documents.
