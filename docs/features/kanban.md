# Kanban Board

A simple drag-and-drop board to visualize your research tasks.

---

## What It Is

A Kanban board shows tasks as cards across columns like To Do → Doing → Done. Move cards as you work to see progress at a glance.

---

## How to Use

1. Click **Kanban** in the sidebar
2. Columns you’ll see: To Do, Reading, Analysis, Writing, Review, Done
3. Click **+ Add Task** at the bottom of any column
4. Give it a title and optional description
5. Optionally set a due date, tags, or attach a saved paper
6. Drag cards between columns as you work

---

## Tips

- Keep tasks small (“Read Smith 2024”) not huge (“Write paper”)
- Limit cards in active columns to 3–5 to avoid multitasking
- Attach resources so you don’t lose the PDF link
- Review the board daily to stay on track
