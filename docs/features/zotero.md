# Zotero Integration

Apex Scholar can connect to your Zotero library to import references directly into your Resource Library.

---

## Connecting Zotero

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Click **Connect to Zotero**
3. Authorize Apex Scholar in the Zotero popup
4. Once connected, your Zotero collections will be available for import

---

## Importing References

1. Navigate to **Resources** (Resource Library)
2. Click the **Import** button
3. Select **From Zotero**
4. Choose the collections you want to import
5. Click **Import**

Your references will be added to your library with metadata and links.

---

## Managing Connection

In **Settings** → **Integrations** you can:

- View connection status
- Disconnect Zotero (revokes access)
- Reconnect if needed

---

## Notes

- Only metadata and links are imported; PDFs remain in Zotero unless you manually upload them to Apex Scholar
- Imports are incremental: previously imported items are skipped on subsequent imports
- Large libraries may take a moment to sync

---

## Zotero API Key

Apex Scholar uses OAuth to connect to Zotero. No API key is required from you—just authorize the app.

---

## Troubleshooting

- If the connection fails, ensure you are logged into Zotero and pop-ups are not blocked
- Check that your Zotero library is not private or restricted
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting
