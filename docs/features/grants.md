# Grant & Funding Tracker

Never miss a proposal deadline. Keep all your grant applications, scholarships, and funded projects in one place.

---

## What It Is

A simple table that tracks:

- Title and funder
- Submission deadline (with countdown)
- Status (Draft, Submitted, Awarded, etc.)
- Checklist of required items
- Links to documents and notes

---

## Adding a Grant

1. Click **Funding** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ New Grant**
3. Fill in the fields
4. Click **Save**

You’ll see it in the table with a “Days remaining” indicator.

---

## Using the Checklist

For each grant, create a list of things you need to submit:

- [ ] Research plan
- [ ] Budget justification
- [ ] CV
- [ ] Letter of support
- [ ] Data management plan

Check items off as you complete them. The list helps ensure nothing is forgotten.

---

## Monitoring Deadlines

- Red: deadline today or past
- Orange: within 7 days
- Green: more than a week

Set internal deadlines earlier than the official one to build buffer.

---

## Notes

The tracker is informational—it doesn’t send email reminders yet. Consider adding due dates to your calendar separately.
