# Citation Engine

Generate formatted citations for any saved paper in seconds.

---

## What It Does

When you have a paper saved in Resources, you can instantly get its citation in:

- BibTeX (for LaTeX)
- APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, Vancouver

Copy to clipboard and paste into your manuscript.

---

## How to Cite

1. Go to **Resources**
2. Find the paper
3. Click the **quote icon**
4. Select your desired style from the dropdown
5. Click **Copy**
6. Paste into your document

---

## Accuracy Tips

- The citation depends on the metadata from the original search source
- If authors or title look wrong, edit the resource’s metadata first (pencil icon)
- Always double-check against the publisher’s version—small punctuation errors can happen

---

## Bulk Export

Currently, citations are generated one by one. Exporting your entire library as a `.bib` file is coming soon. For now, use **Settings → Export Backup** and convert the JSON with a script (advanced).
