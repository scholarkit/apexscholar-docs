## Welcome, Researcher!

Apex Scholar is designed to give you back your time. Instead of switching between Zotero, Google Scholar, Word, Excel, and Trello, everything you need is in one clean dashboard.

### What can you do here?

- **Discover** relevant papers without opening ten tabs
- **Save and organize** your library by project
- **Let AI summarize** abstracts so you can skim faster
- **See the big picture** with a visual knowledge graph
- **Write papers** with built-in LaTeX compilation
- **Track deadlines** for grants and tasks
- **Generate citations** instantly in any format

And it’s free and open-source.

---

## Getting Started is Easy

1. **Sign up** with Puter (cloud storage) – no password to remember
2. **Create a project** for your current research focus
3. **Search** for papers using the Explore tab
4. **Save** interesting papers to your library
5. **Review** AI-extracted insights on the Insights page

That’s it—you’re ready to research smarter.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>Do I need to install software?</summary>
No. You can use Apex Scholar right in your browser at <a href="https://apex-scholar.vercel.app">apex-scholar.vercel.app</a>. If you prefer to host your own instance, we provide simple installation instructions.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is my data safe?</summary>
Yes. When you enable End-to-End Encryption, your data is encrypted before it leaves your browser. Not even we can read it. You can also self-host for full control.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Which databases can I search?</summary>
ArXiv, Google Scholar, PubMed, Semantic Scholar, and OpenAlex—all from one search box.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I collaborate with my team?</summary>
Collaboration features are coming soon. Today, Apex Scholar is optimized for individual researchers.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What about LaTeX? Do I need to install TeX Live?</summary>
No. The built-in LaTeX editor compiles your documents in the cloud via GitHub Actions. Just write and click Compile.
</details>
